Prajwal Vijay is a 3rd year Electrical Engineering undergraduate at IIT Madras with research interests in Robot Learning, Manipulation, Control Systems, and Multi-Agent Systems. He is collaborating with the Personal Robotics Lab at the University of Washington on multi-modal data extraction for robotics and with IISc's AIRL Lab on geometry-adaptive engagement algorithms for perimeter defence. His previous work includes the Colosseum V2 benchmark for VLA generalization at USC, Multi-Player Reach-Avoid Differential Games at IIT Madras, and Inverse Kinematics and Autonomy with Team Anveshak, IIT Madras's Mars Rover team.
